## Deliverables

### 1. Forge Council Faction File
**Assignee:** Writer1
**File:** `worlds/wylderan/vault/Compendium/Factions/Forge Council.md`
**Scope:** 120-150 lines (full faction template)

**Content Sections:**
1. **Overview**
   - Founded ~22 years ago by guild masters + landowners to resist lowland creditor expansion
   - Myra's restructure 20 years ago after harsh winter (centralized power, made smith seats permanent)
   - Current structure: 5-member body, guild-dominated, Myra as council head for 20 years

2. **Leadership** (5-member council)
   - **[[Myra Cadros]]** - Council Head, master smith, Cadros Ironworks, 20 years in power
   - **[[Hal Brennan]]** - Master smith, Brennan's Forge, Myra's rival, quiet underminer
   - **[[Aldric Harth]]** - Landowner rep, grain lord, in debt to Corwin Marsh, compromised
   - **Jorin Kelm** - Landowner rep, Myra loyalist, Cadros customer, conservative (2-3 sentences)
   - **Marna Veld** - Landowner rep, swing vote, timber yards neighbor, pragmatic (2-3 sentences)

3. **Structure & Governance**
   - 2 master smith seats (permanent) + 3 landowner reps (elected, 3-year terms, staggered)
   - Landowner eligibility: 50+ acres, guild-affiliated (excludes independent/tenant farmers)
   - Majority vote (3 of 5), unanimous for structural changes
   - Myra controls vote (herself + Brennan + Jorin = 3)
   - Monthly meetings at Stone Hearth second-floor hall
   - Quorum: 4 of 5 members

4. **Territory & Jurisdiction**
   - Ashcrag settlement (~350 pop) + outlying farms within "Ashcrag valley watershed" (Cold Run, Ashwater, Piney Flow drainage)
   - Includes farms, timber ops, charcoal kilns feeding Ashcrag economy
   - Does NOT include Ironhollow (far side of ridgeline, cartel company town since ~10 years ago)
   - Northern boundary: Ridgeline separating from Ironhollow
   - Southern boundary: The Confluence (where three streams merge to form Millrun River)

5. **Resources & Assets**
   - Market fees: 10% on all transactions, collected by Grett Voss, ~500-600 gold/year
   - Barge loading priority: Council sets schedule, weapon against non-compliant farmers
   - Grain price influence: Manipulate supply via loading control (stabilize or punish)
   - Timber licensing: Can revoke for "unsafe practices" (vague, political enforcement)
   - Emergency grain reserve: 50 tons stored, Myra drawing from it secretly (shortage pressure)
   - Does NOT control: Forge output (privately owned), land (private estates), charcoal supply (timber families control)

6. **Goals & Motivations**
   - Maintain highland independence from lowland influence (Assembly, Crown)
   - Preserve guild control over economy (guild-run governance = stability in Myra's view)
   - Expand forge trade, reclaim Ironhollow market from cartel tools
   - Maintain emergency grain reserve (prevent another harsh winter starvation)

7. **Opposition**
   - **Highland Merchant Cartel**: Economic rivalry, capital vs production control, Ironhollow displacement
   - **Highland Workers Coalition**: Farmer strike targets council (representation, land reform, reduced fees), 15 committed of 40
   - **Saltmere Merchant Assembly**: Aldric's debt (due in 2 months), lowland leverage over highland grain
   - **Crown Authority** (potential): Nominal fealty now, could intervene if lowland creditors push for it

8. **Internal Tensions**
   - **Myra vs Aldric**: Guild interest vs landowner desperation, Aldric smuggling to pay debt
   - **Guild vs Landowners**: 2 smith seats vs 3 landowner seats creates structural tension
   - **Myra vs Brennan**: Quiet rivalry, Brennan votes with Myra publicly but undermines privately
   - **Coalition response divide**: Myra wants to wait, Aldric wants immediate crackdown, Brennan waiting to see which way wind blows

9. **Counter-Insurgency Stance**
   - Myra knows about HWC organizing, has not decided response
   - Current strategy: Wait for coalition to collapse, prepare economic pressure (barge denial, timber license revocation)
   - Informant network: Thom Harrow reports to Tallow Breck (cartel), Grett Voss monitors market activity
   - Weakness: Council doesn't know about Ironhollow-Ashcrag coordination (Senna-Orrin link), thinks it's just local farmer complaints
   - Trigger points: Strike declaration, sabotage, violence = immediate crackdown

10. **Crown Relationship**
   - Nominal fealty: Annual rep sent to renew fealty (ceremonial, no substance)
   - De facto independence: Crown does not tax, enforce, or govern highlands in living memory
   - Why: Highlands too remote/poor after Theomachis, Crown focused on core territories
   - Fragility: If Assembly seizes highland grain, Crown might back lowland claim to legitimize it

11. **Campaign Role**
   - Obstacle to labor reform (refuse coalition demands, maintain guild control)
   - Potential ally against cartel if PCs approach correctly (shared interest: highland independence)
   - Political quest hub: Council votes, internal faction maneuvering, PC negotiation
   - Power shift scenario: If Aldric defaults, Assembly controls grain, council loses stability

**Source Material:**
- All 3 workshop files
- Ashcrag.md (governance structure, layout, tensions)
- Myra Cadros.md (founding crisis, restructure, council head backstory)
- Aldric Harth.md (debt crisis, landowner perspective)
- Highland Workers Coalition.md (opposition dynamics, council awareness)
