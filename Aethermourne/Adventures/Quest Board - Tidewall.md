# Quest Board: Tidewall

*The beating heart of the [[The Hollowed Reach]], where coin talks and the tide listens.*

---

## 1. The Bilges Disappearances

> [!info] Quest Details
> | | |
> |---|---|
> | **Quest Giver** | [[Sergeant Bryn Kellar]], Tidewall City Watch |
> | **Objective** | Investigate the disappearance of 14 residents from the Bilges district over the past month |
> | **Difficulty** | Moderate |
> | **Reward** | 200 gold, City Watch commendation, access to restricted harbor records |
> | **Complication** | The disappearances are connected to [[Elara Duskmantle]]'s property purchases above sea caves. She has Concord protection. |

**Hook:** "Fourteen people gone from the Bilges in four weeks. The Watch says runaways. The families say otherwise. Kellar is the only officer who cares, and she's running out of time before the case is closed."

> [!warning]- GM Only
> The missing people are being lured into sea caves beneath Duskmantle's properties, where [[The Veil Unbound]] is conducting rituals to amplify [[The Twelve#Serith\|Serith's]] whispers. Several victims are alive but Sleepwalking. Duskmantle is not Veil Unbound herself, she is a profiteer selling access to the caves. Following the money leads to [[Cordell Raith]], Master of Secrets, who is facilitating the operation.

## 2. The Salt Preacher

> [!info] Quest Details
> | | |
> |---|---|
> | **Quest Giver** | [[Keeper Lira Ashvane]], Lantern-Keepers |
> | **Objective** | Find and safely detain "Salt," a former Sleepwalker preaching about voices beneath the waves |
> | **Difficulty** | Low |
> | **Reward** | 80 gold, Lantern-Keeper favor, information about Sleepwalker patterns |
> | **Complication** | Salt is gathering a following. Detaining them risks a riot. They also know something genuinely useful about Serith's prison. |

**Hook:** "A wild-eyed figure called Salt is preaching on the docks every night, drawing crowds, telling them about the voice beneath the trench. Ashvane wants them brought in quietly before the Concord decides to do it loudly."

> [!warning]- GM Only
> Salt is a former Sleepwalker who retained fragments of [[The Twelve#Serith\|Serith's]] communication. Their preaching is garbled but contains real intelligence about the Abyssal Trench prison. The Lantern-Keepers want them contained for study. [[The Veil Unbound]] wants them recruited. The Concord wants them silenced. The party must choose who gets access to what Salt knows.

## 3. Black Tide Salvage

> [!info] Quest Details
> | | |
> |---|---|
> | **Quest Giver** | [[Admiral Aldren Vayne]], Master of Ships |
> | **Objective** | Recover cargo from a merchant vessel lost to Black Tide in the Outer Reach |
> | **Difficulty** | High |
> | **Reward** | 500 gold, a share of recovered cargo, Vayne's personal backing for one political favor |
> | **Complication** | The ship is not wrecked. It is adrift, intact, crewed by Sleepwalkers who are defending it against all approach. |

**Hook:** "The *Silver Current* went dark three days ago in Black Tide waters. Vayne's sending a recovery crew, but his sailors won't go near Black Tide. He needs people who aren't afraid of what the tide leaves behind."

> [!warning]- GM Only
> The *Silver Current* was carrying a sealed crate from the [[The Star-Readers\|Star-Readers]]' archive, a coded Age of Bones document describing [[The Twelve#Yvenne\|Yvenne's]] original method for constructing Serith's prison. The Black Tide infected the crew but left the crate untouched, Serith's agents want the document intact. The Sleepwalker crew is guarding it on her orders.

## 4. The Tidemarket Forgery Ring

> [!info] Quest Details
> | | |
> |---|---|
> | **Quest Giver** | [[Jessamine Kole]], Master of Coin |
> | **Objective** | Identify and shut down a ring producing counterfeit trade seals, undermining Tidewall's currency |
> | **Difficulty** | Moderate |
> | **Reward** | 300 gold, Kole's gratitude (politically valuable), seized forgery equipment |
> | **Complication** | The ring is run by [[Fenn Dalloway]], Keeper of the Isles, a sitting Tidekeeper, using the profits to fund [[The Veil Unbound]]. |

**Hook:** "Counterfeit trade seals are flooding the Tidemarket. The economy is wobbling. Kole suspects an inside job but cannot investigate her own colleagues without proof. She needs outsiders."

> [!warning]- GM Only
> [[Fenn Dalloway]] is laundering money through the forgery ring to fund Veil Unbound operations across the Reach. Exposing him triggers a political crisis in the Concord, potentially unseating a Tidekeeper. [[Cordell Raith]] knows about the ring and is protecting Dalloway in exchange for a cut. The party can expose both or leverage the information for political power.

## 5. The Archive Anomaly

> [!info] Quest Details
> | | |
> |---|---|
> | **Quest Giver** | [[Arch-Reader Celestine Vael]], Star-Readers |
> | **Objective** | Investigate a sealed vault in the Archive of Tides that has begun emitting light and sound |
> | **Difficulty** | Moderate |
> | **Reward** | 150 gold, Star-Reader research access, a minor divination focus item |
> | **Complication** | The vault contains pre-Silence star maps that are updating themselves, showing stars that have already gone dark reigniting in a pattern that should not be possible. |

**Hook:** "The sealed vault in the Archive has been quiet for centuries. Last night it started humming. This morning the door was warm. Celestine Vael needs someone to open it, and she does not trust the Concord's people to do it without confiscating the contents."

> [!warning]- GM Only
> The star maps are responding to changes in the Loom of Fate's structure. The pattern of reigniting stars traces the shape of the counter-prophecy's influence spreading from [[The Loom's Edge]]. This is early evidence that the excised fate-threads are reaching beyond the Unwritten Lands. Celestine does not know what the pattern means, but the party, if they later visit the Loom's Edge, will recognize it.

## 6. Harren Coyle's Favor

> [!info] Quest Details
> | | |
> |---|---|
> | **Quest Giver** | [[Harren Coyle]], tavern owner and information broker |
> | **Objective** | Retrieve a stolen ledger from a rival broker operating from a ship in the harbor |
> | **Difficulty** | Low |
> | **Reward** | No gold, but Coyle's information network opens to the party: three free intelligence requests |
> | **Complication** | The rival broker is an Oldborn agent from [[The Unwritten Lands]], the first confirmed Oldborn spy operating on the known continent. |

**Hook:** "Coyle leans across the bar and speaks quietly: someone lifted his ledger of contacts. Not a common thief, someone who knew exactly where to look. He wants it back before his network is compromised. Payment is in kind: stories for stories, favors for favors."

> [!warning]- GM Only
> The Oldborn spy, operating under the name "Kess," was sent by [[Speaker Yavenne]]'s Open Hand faction to gather intelligence about the known continent. The ledger theft was opportunistic. Kess is willing to negotiate: return the ledger in exchange for a meeting with the party. This is the first thread connecting Tidewall to the Unwritten Lands arc.
