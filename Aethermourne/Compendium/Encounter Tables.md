# Random Encounter Tables

*Roll when the party travels between locations, rests in dangerous territory, or when you need to break up a long scene. Not every encounter is combat. The best encounters reveal something about the region.*

---

*Solvaen's bones impose order. But order cracks.*

|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | A patrol of [[Order of the Ashen Flame]] soldiers demands papers and inspects the party's belongings. They are looking for contraband Ashite. | Low | Social | [[Commander Ashara Voss]] |
| 2 | An [[Ash Revenants\|Ash Revenant]] kneels at a crossroads shrine, whispering prayers to [[The Twelve#Solvaen\|Solvaen]] in a voice like grinding stone. It has not noticed the party. It will attack if disturbed. | Moderate | Combat | [[Ash Revenants]] |
| 3 | A member of the [[Unbound Congregation]] approaches in disguise, offering information about [[The Cinders\|Cinder]] movements in exchange for safe passage to the border. | Low | Social | [[Elowen Greaves]] |

| 5 | A [[Hollow Ones\|Hollow One]] stands motionless in a field, staring at nothing. Farmers give it a wide berth. If the party investigates, it speaks a single sentence of prophecy before collapsing into ash. | Moderate | Mystery | [[Hollow Ones]] |
| 6 | A Cinder informant is following the party. Perceptive characters notice the same face at the last two rest stops. Confrontation reveals they report to [[Delric Mourne]]. | Moderate | Social | [[The Cinders]] |

| 8 | Three [[Ash Typhons]] circle overhead, drawn by a dying Ashite crystal embedded in a roadside shrine. They will attack anyone who approaches the crystal. | High | Combat | [[Ash Typhons]] |
