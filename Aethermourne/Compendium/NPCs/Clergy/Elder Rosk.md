# Elder Rosk

|||
|---|---|
| **Title** | Eldest Greensinger |
| **Location** | [[The Verdant Marches#Roothold|Roothold]] (Greensinger Grove), [[The Verdant Marches]] |
| **Faction** | [[The Greensingers]] |
| **Status** | Alive (very old) |

---

**Description.** An ancient elf, over 800 years old, withered and bent, with bark-like skin and eyes of deep amber. Small fungi and moss grow in his hair. He moves slowly but his mind is sharp. Rosk has communed with the land longer than anyone alive. He knows the corruption is real, he's sensed "shadow in the roots", but doesn't know its source. He is patient, cryptic, and occasionally frustrating.

> [!warning]- GM Notes
> **Campaign Role.** Guide and information source in Act III. He directs the PCs toward the corruption's heart. His knowledge of [[The Twelve#Thyrea|Thyrea]]'s lingering consciousness is essential.
>
> **Roleplay Notes.** Speaks in metaphor and long silences. Answers questions with questions. Hums tunelessly when thinking. Despite the cryptic manner, he is not withholding information, he genuinely perceives reality in ways that are difficult to translate into words.
