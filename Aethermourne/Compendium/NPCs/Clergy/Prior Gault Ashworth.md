# Prior Gault Ashworth

**Church Pilgrimage Master** | [[Crown's Reach]] | [[Order of the Ashen Flame]]

---

## Appearance

Sixties, tall and gaunt, with the geometric Ashite-ink tattoos of the Anointed caste on his forehead. White robes, immaculate. His eyes are pale grey and unnervingly steady, the look of a man who has climbed The Crown twice and come down certain.

## Personality

A true believer. Gault has dedicated his life to the Church and genuinely believes [[The Twelve#Solvaen|Solvaen's]] order is divine truth. He is not corrupt, not cruel, not a hypocrite. He is a good man inside a bad system, which makes him more dangerous than a villain, because he legitimizes the system's worst features with his sincerity.

He manages pilgrimages with meticulous care and genuine concern for the pilgrims' wellbeing.

## What He Wants

- **Service.** To fulfil his duty to the Church faithfully.
- **Understanding.** The Crown's Hum has changed pitch. He noticed, he reported it, and the Church told him to stop asking.

## Connections

- [[Order of the Ashen Flame]], devoted member
- [[Crown's Reach]], his posting
- Pilgrims of the Spine

---

> [!warning]- GM Only
> Gault's second climb left him with an unshakeable conviction that something beneath the Spine is wrong. He cannot articulate what, only that the certainty he brought down from The Crown includes a warning he doesn't understand.
>
> He has begun keeping a private journal of unusual observations. This journal could be valuable to PCs investigating the Spine. Gault will not part with it easily, but a sincere appeal to his faith (Solvaen's order demands truth) could persuade him.
