# Tessara Wildbloom

**Greensinger** | The Deep Cradle (missing) | [[The Greensingers]] | [[The Verdant Marches]]

---

## Appearance

Forties (when last seen), lean, with the deep-green eyes and bark-textured skin patches common to experienced Greensingers. Vines had begun growing into her hair naturally. Now she sits cross-legged at the base of a Cradle tree, eyes open, breathing, apparently alive, but not present.

## Personality (Before Disappearance)

Brave, curious, methodical. Tessara pushed her communion deeper than safe to identify the corruption [[Elder Rosk]] sensed. She found it.

## What She Wants

To come home.

## Connections

- [[The Greensingers]], her order
- [[Elder Rosk]], sent her to investigate
- [[Kaelith Thornborn]], whose channel the corruption is heading toward

---

> [!warning]- GM Only
> Tessara's consciousness is suspended in the corrupted root-network, trapped by [[The Twelve#Serith|Serith's]] influence which feeds on her connection to [[The Twelve#Thyrea|Thyrea's]] essence. The land around her communion site has gone wrong: spiral dark growth, black leaves, brine smell.
>
> **Rescue requirements:**
> - Physically reaching her deep in the Cradle (dangerous terrain, corrupted wildlife)
> - Having a Greensinger guide her consciousness out through communion
>
> **What she carries:** Critical intel. The corruption is entropic, from deep beneath the earth, heading toward [[Thornhaven]] and [[Kaelith Thornborn]]'s channel. This is some of the most direct evidence of Serith's influence available in the Marches storyline.
