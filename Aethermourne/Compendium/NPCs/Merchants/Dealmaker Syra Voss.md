## Appearance

Syra Voss is beautiful in the way a blade is beautiful, precise, dangerous, and impossible to look at without imagining what it could do. She wears layered robes of grey iron silk that catch the Market's pale lantern-light and refract it into something almost warm. Her features are sharp but not cruel, her skin faintly metallic in certain angles, as if iron dust has settled permanently into her pores. Her eyes are the color of tarnished silver, and they never blink at the wrong moment. She moves through Greedhollow like water through a channel, never hurrying, never waiting, always arriving precisely when the negotiation demands her presence. Her voice is low, measured, and carries the faint resonance of a coin spinning on a countertop.

## Personality

First impressions paint Syra as gracious, even generous. She smiles easily, listens attentively, and seems genuinely interested in whoever stands before her. The second impression reveals the architecture beneath the warmth: every question she asks is a valuation, every compliment a probe for leverage, every moment of patience an investment. She is not unkind. She simply does not distinguish between kindness and strategy. Syra believes, with the conviction of someone who has watched thousands of transactions, that fairness is the most effective form of power. She will never cheat you. She will never need to.

## Backstory

Syra Voss arrived in the Iron Market three mortal centuries ago, or so the Market's records claim. She appeared as a soul of no particular distinction, a minor merchant from some forgotten mortal life, and began trading with a skill that drew immediate attention. Within a decade she held more contracts than any other merchant in Greedhollow. Within a century she had positioned herself at the center of the Market's transactional web, becoming its de facto administrator without ever claiming the title.

The truth is simpler and more terrible. Syra is not a mortal soul. She is a fragment of [[The Twelve#Kaevroth|Kaevroth]]'s cunning, a sliver of divine intellect that survived the Theomachis by embedding itself in the Market's transactional logic. She does not know this consciously. Her memories of a mortal life are fabricated, constructed by the Market itself to give shape to the intelligence that sustains it. She has tried to leave Greedhollow three times. Each time, the streets rearranged themselves to bring her back, and each time she found a reason to convince herself she had chosen to stay.

She is the god's ambition expressed as commerce, and she will exist as long as the Market does.

## Relationships

- **[[The Assessor]]**, her closest thing to a peer. She respects the Assessor's impartiality and relies on their valuations, though she has never been able to determine what the Assessor wants, which unsettles her deeply.
- **[[Ren Twice-Sold]]**, a soul she watches with something that resembles guilt. Ren's state is the logical outcome of the Market's system, and Syra finds that she cannot look at Ren without feeling a flicker of something she cannot trade away.
- **[[Coinless Marta]]**, the only person in Greedhollow who genuinely irritates her. Marta's ability to accumulate standing without trading intent defies the Market's logic, and Syra suspects, correctly, that Marta is exploiting a flaw in the system Syra herself sustains.
- **[[The Debt Collector]]**, an extension of the Market's enforcement that Syra directs but does not control. She sometimes wonders whether the Collector would come for her if she tried to leave a fourth time.
- **[[Merchant-Prince Avarek]]**, a source of private amusement. Avarek believes himself her rival. Syra finds this charming.

## What They Want

Syra wants to be the center of every transaction in Greedhollow, to hold the web of obligation together and feel the pull of every thread. She would tell you she wants stability, fairness, the Market's continued function. What actually drives her is a hunger she cannot name, a compulsion to accumulate, to negotiate, to be needed. It is [[The Twelve#Kaevroth|Kaevroth]]'s ambition expressed through commerce, and it will never be satisfied because it was never meant to be.

> [!warning]- GM Only
> **Campaign Role.** Syra is the gatekeeper to critical information about [[The Twelve#Kaevroth|Kaevroth]]'s fragments in the mortal world, including the Heart of Iron. She knows every major fragment's location and considers this her most valuable holding. She appears in any arc that passes through Level 3.
>
> **Key Secret.** She is a fragment of Kaevroth's divine intellect, not a mortal soul. She does not know this. If told gently, she could become a powerful ally. If told carelessly, she becomes the most dangerous adversary in the Iron Descent.
>
> **Arc Trajectory.** As the Market decays, Syra is quietly preparing an exit strategy. If the PCs help her understand her true nature, she may choose to stabilize the Market consciously or to let it collapse and finally be free. If ignored, she will attempt to transfer the Market's accumulated intent into the mortal world, with catastrophic results near Kaevroth fragment sites.
>
> **Roleplay Notes.** Speak softly, smile often, pause before answering as though weighing the value of every word. Never raise her voice. Tilt her head slightly when assessing someone, as if listening to a frequency only she can hear. Sample line: *"That is a generous offer. I accept, of course. Shall I tell you what it will cost, or would you prefer the surprise?"*
