# Dust-Trader Oren Blackfinger

|||
|---|---|
| **Title** | Master Dust-Trader |
| **Location** | [[Riftmarket]], [[The Shattered Edge]] |
| **Faction** | Independent, loose [[The Ember Guild|Ember Guild]] affiliation |
| **Status** | Alive |

---

## Appearance

Forties, stocky, with ruddy skin, a square jaw, and a merchant's easy smile that does not quite reach his eyes. His hands are his signature: fingers stained permanently black from decades of handling Vorrhyn's Dust, the discoloration extending past his wrists and creeping up his forearms like ink soaking into cloth. The staining is not merely cosmetic. Prolonged Dust exposure has saturated his skin with trace chaotic energy, and small objects in his immediate vicinity occasionally change, a coin flipping to a different face, a button shifting color, a pen bending slightly when he sets it down. Oren ignores these effects with the practiced indifference of a man who decided years ago that profit outweighed concern.

He dresses well, the best-tailored clothes in [[Riftmarket]], in dark colors that hide the staining on his sleeves.

---

## Personality

Shrewd, gregarious, and morally flexible. Oren is a born trader who understands that on the Shattered Edge, survival and commerce are the same thing. He is generous with his friends, fair with his customers, and ruthless with his competitors. He does not cheat, because cheating is bad for long-term business, but he will exploit every advantage the market offers, including advantages that would make a more principled person uncomfortable.

He is not a bad man. He is a practical one. The distinction matters on the Edge, where rigid morality is a luxury the environment does not support.

Beneath the charm, Oren is worried. The Dust trade has been his life, and the patterns are changing. New buyers with deep pockets and unclear purposes. Unusual demand for Empyrean Shards. Questions being asked that suggest someone wants to do something with chaotic materials that goes beyond alchemy or scholarship. Oren's survival instincts are excellent, and they are telling him something is wrong.

---

## Backstory

Oren arrived at the Shattered Edge fifteen years ago as a junior [[The Ember Guild|Ember Guild]] trader, assigned to the Riftmarket outpost because no one else wanted the posting. He discovered two things: that Vorrhyn's Dust was spectacularly undervalued by the wider market, and that he had a knack for handling it. Most traders cannot work with Dust directly because the chaotic energy causes disorientation. Oren found it invigorating. He built relationships with Drift-harvesters, Threshold runners, and Shimmer Zone gatherers, cutting out intermediaries and establishing himself as the primary wholesaler in Riftmarket.

The Ember Guild considers him a freelancer now, useful but not controllable. He maintains the affiliation because Guild networks give him access to buyers across Aethermourne, but his loyalty is to himself and to Riftmarket, in roughly that order.

---

## Relationships

- **[[Tyrell Vane]]**: Mutual respect layered over mutual suspicion. Vane values Oren's economic intelligence. Oren respects Vane's leadership. Neither fully trusts the other, which is probably healthy.
- **[[The Ember Guild]]**: Former employer, current business partner. The Guild provides distribution networks. Oren provides product. The arrangement works because both sides profit.
- **[[The Veil Unbound]]**: Unknown to Oren, several of his newer customers are Veil Unbound intermediaries purchasing Empyrean Shards. He has noticed the unusual buying patterns but has not yet connected them to a larger agenda.
- **Drift-harvesters**: Oren's suppliers, the risk-takers who gather Dust and Shards from the outer zones. He pays fairly because dead or resentful harvesters are bad for business.

---

## What He Wants

- **Profit.** Always and first. Oren makes no apologies for this.
- **Answers about the new buyers.** His instincts say something is wrong, and Oren trusts his instincts more than he trusts most people. He wants someone discreet to investigate.
- **A way out, eventually.** Oren knows the Dust exposure is changing him. The effects are still minor, but they are progressing. He tells himself he will retire before it goes too far. He has been telling himself this for five years.

> [!warning]- GM Notes
> **Campaign Role.** Information source and potential quest-giver. Oren's commercial intelligence can point PCs toward the [[The Veil Unbound|Veil Unbound]]'s Empyrean Shard purchases, providing a concrete, investigable thread leading to the cult's Maelstrom ritual. He is not brave enough to investigate himself, but he will fund and support PCs who are.
>
> **The Dust's Cost.** Oren's prolonged Dust exposure is more advanced than he admits. The reality-warping effects around him are intensifying, and he has begun to experience brief moments where he perceives two versions of the same object simultaneously. This is the early stage of a Changing that, if it progresses, will be significant. The question is whether it will happen gently or catastrophically.
>
> **Roleplay Notes.** Speaks like a salesman, warm, expansive, always circling toward the deal. Gestures broadly. Offers drinks. Calls everyone "friend" until they prove otherwise. When the charm drops, usually in private, his voice goes flat and precise, and the shrewd mind behind the smile becomes very visible. He fidgets with objects constantly, and those objects change subtly in his hands.
