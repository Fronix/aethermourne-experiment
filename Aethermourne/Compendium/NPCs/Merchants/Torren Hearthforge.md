**Description.** An ancient dwarf (over 400 years old) with a white beard that reaches his belt, thick spectacles, and hands permanently stained by forge-work. He is the greatest living smith. Gruff, practical, secretly sentimental. He tracks [[The Twelve#Kaevroth|Kaevroth]]'s iron fragments as a personal obsession.

> [!warning]- GM Notes
> **Campaign Role.** Craftsman and information source. Can forge divine-material equipment for the PCs. His knowledge of Kaevroth's fragments may be plot-relevant.
>
> **Roleplay Notes.** Mumbles into his beard. Evaluates people by looking at their gear first, their face second. Will talk for hours about metallurgy if allowed. Calls everyone "lad" or "lass" regardless of age or species. Cries at good craftsmanship.
