# Commander Ashara Voss

---

**Description.** A tall, powerfully built woman in her late forties with short-cropped grey hair and a burn scar across her right cheek. She wears the Order's grey-and-white plate armor at all times. Voss is a true believer, she genuinely believes [[The Twelve#Solvaen|Solvaen]]'s order is the only thing keeping the world from chaos. She is brutal, disciplined, and principled in her own way. She advocates preemptive war against [[The Verdant Marches]].

> [!warning]- GM Notes
> **Campaign Role.** Potential antagonist or uneasy ally in Act II. If the PCs convince her the Cinders are compromised, she becomes a powerful (if uncomfortable) ally. If not, she's an obstacle.
>
> **Roleplay Notes.** Clipped, military diction. Never sits, always stands or paces. Makes direct eye contact that borders on intimidation. Respects strength and conviction; despises evasion.
