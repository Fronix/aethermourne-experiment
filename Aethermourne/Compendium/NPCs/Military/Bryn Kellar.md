## Description

Bryn is in his early thirties, stocky, with ruddy skin and a perpetual sunburn. Red-brown hair kept short under his Keeper's cap. A wide, honest face that makes him look younger than he is. His hands are scarred from rope burns and Trench-creature encounters.

Earnest, brave, and slightly too idealistic for his line of work, Bryn volunteered for [[The Lantern-Keepers]] because he believed in the mission, and three years in, he still believes, which makes him rare among the rank and file. He's good at his job, solid in a fight, steady in the fog, and absolutely terrible at politics. He says what he thinks, which endears him to his squad and infuriates his superiors.

**What he wants:** To do the right thing. To protect the coast. To understand why his latest discovery, a stone altar in a tidal cave that appeared overnight, makes him feel like someone is watching him even when he's alone.

> [!warning]- GM Only
> Kellar is the ideal "ground-level" ally for PCs in [[Veil Harbor]]. He's competent, trustworthy, and connected to events through his patrol route.
>
> His discovery of the altar is a hook that ties to [[The Veil Unbound|the Veil Unbound's]] ritual network. If the PCs follow his lead, they'll find a trail of ritual sites along the coast.
>
> Kellar is also being watched by the Veil Unbound, who know he found the altar. They haven't moved against him yet because killing a Lantern-Keeper draws attention.
