# Warden Draven Ironbark

|||
|---|---|
| **Title** | Marchwarden of Clan Ironbark |
| **Location** | Northern [[The Verdant Marches]] |
| **Faction** | Ironbark Opposition ([[Marchwardens Council|Marchwardens' Council]]) |
| **Status** | Alive |

---

**Description.** A massive, scarred man in his fifties with a braided red beard and arms like tree trunks. He wears ironwood armor and carries a massive axe. Draven is a traditionalist, he believes the Marches' strength is in their independence, and any unification is just tyranny wearing a friendly face. He's not wrong, exactly, but his stubbornness could doom the Marches if unity is needed.

> [!warning]- GM Notes
> **Campaign Role.** Potential obstacle or ally in Act III. If the PCs can earn his respect (through strength, not words), he becomes a grudging supporter. If not, he actively undermines [[Kaelith Thornborn|Kaelith]].
>
> **Roleplay Notes.** Loud, blunt, confrontational. Tests people by provoking them, he respects those who push back without losing control. Drinks heavily but never seems impaired. Surprisingly gentle with children and animals.
