**Description.** A weathered woman in her forties with short auburn hair streaked with grey, sea-green eyes, and hands calloused from rope and weapon alike. She wears the Lantern-Keepers' salt-stained grey coat with a single lantern pin at the collar. Ashvane is war-weary but unbroken, she has fought the deep's horrors for twenty years and will fight for twenty more if needed. She is honest, direct, and desperately in need of capable people.

> [!warning]- GM Notes
> **Campaign Role.** The campaign's primary patron in Act I. She gives the PCs their first missions, provides resources and information, and becomes a trusted ally throughout.
>
> **Roleplay Notes.** No-nonsense delivery. Says exactly what she means. Pauses before speaking, weighs her words. Shows trust through action, not warmth. If she sends the PCs somewhere dangerous, she'll be honest about the risk.
