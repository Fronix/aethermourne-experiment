## Appearance

Merren was a lean, sharp-featured woman in her thirties with close-cropped auburn hair and Vigil tattoos on her forearms. In death, her skin has gone grey-white, her eyes the flat silver of frozen water. The wounds that killed her, claw marks across her torso, have not healed and never will. She still wears her [[The Ashen Vigil|Vigil]] armor, battered and stained with old blood, and carries herself like a soldier on a patrol that has lasted eighteen months. She will not remove her armor. If asked about her wounds, she changes the subject.

## Personality

In life, Merren was the [[The Ashen Vigil|Vigil's]] best scout: sharp, decisive, brave to the point of recklessness. Death taught her patience. She retains her intelligence and mission focus, but she is more cautious now, careful in the way of someone who has learned that dying once was enough. She is frightened, because Remnants can feel fear, and exhausted in the psychological sense of someone who has spent a year and a half hiding from an army while carrying intelligence that could save the Wastes.

## Backstory

Merren grew up near [[Lastlight]] and joined the [[The Ashen Vigil|Vigil]] young, drawn by the clarity of its mission: hold the line, protect the living, kill the dead. She earned her scout-captain rank through skill and nerve, leading reconnaissance patrols into the [[The Pale Wastes#The Hollowdeep|Hollowdeep's]] outer reaches where other scouts refused to go.

When [[Thessa Ironveil]] ordered deep reconnaissance into the crater's interior, Merren volunteered. Her scouting party was overwhelmed by organized undead, formations that moved with tactical purpose rather than mindless hunger. She died in the fighting. She rose as a Remnant.

For eighteen months she has survived alone in the crater's periphery, hiding from patrols that guard the rim, observing everything. She has watched the undead drilling in military formations. She has seen structures being built from bone and frozen earth. She has felt the skull's energy change to something rhythmic and purposeful. She carved a warning into divine bone and sent it toward [[Greyhold]]: *"Merren Vask lives. The quiet has teeth."* She cannot leave the crater because organized patrols guard the rim. She is waiting for someone to come in and find her.

## Relationships

- **[[Thessa Ironveil]]**, her commander, the woman who sent her in. Merren does not blame Ironveil. The mission was necessary. She worries about how Ironveil is handling the guilt of fifty-four lost scouts.
- **[[Bjorn Ashken]]**, the Holdwarden she is trying to reach. Her bone-carved message was meant for him: a warning from the only survivor who has seen the army from the inside.
- **[[Revenant Yael]]**, Merren has never met Yael, but she knows of the [[Remnants Court|Remnants' Court]]. The knowledge that there is a place for people like her gives her something to hold onto.
- **The lost scouts**, the other fifty-three who went into the [[The Pale Wastes#The Hollowdeep|Hollowdeep]] and did not return. Merren does not know if any survived. Finding out is one of the things keeping her sane.

## What They Want

To deliver her warning to [[Bjorn Ashken]] and [[Thessa Ironveil]]. To be accepted as herself, not as a monster. To find out whether any of her scouts survived. And, quietly, to stop hiding.

> [!warning]- GM Only
> **Campaign Role.** Intelligence source in Act IV. If the party reaches the [[The Pale Wastes#The Hollowdeep|Hollowdeep]] periphery, Merren is the first friendly contact they find. She provides critical tactical information about the undead army's organization, the structures being built around [[The Twelve#Morrhael|Morrhael's]] skull, and the changes in the crater's energy patterns. Her intelligence could change the war's outcome.
>
> **Key Secret.** The undead army is not a horde. It is an organized military force with formations, patrols, supply lines of necromantic energy, and a command structure radiating from the skull. Merren has mapped patrol routes, identified weak points in the rim guard, and estimated force numbers. She carries this information in her memory and in scratches on the inside of her armor.
>
> **Arc Trajectory.** Begins as a survivor waiting for rescue. Once found, she becomes an invaluable tactical asset, a scout who knows the Hollowdeep's interior better than anyone alive or dead. If the PCs bring her back to [[Greyhold]], she becomes the moral test: a Vigil officer who swore to destroy the undead, who is now undead herself. Some Holds would destroy her on sight. Her information could save thousands, but delivering it requires the living to trust a dead woman.
>
> **Roleplay Notes.** Speaks in a low, careful voice, the habit of someone who has spent eighteen months avoiding detection. Holds herself at a distance from the living, not out of hostility, but because she knows what she looks like. Still uses Vigil hand signals instinctively, sometimes before words. Counts things compulsively, exits, patrol intervals, heartbeats she no longer has. Sample line: *"The dead are drilling. Formations of a hundred, shoulder to shoulder, moving in lockstep. I watched them for three months before I believed what I was seeing."*
