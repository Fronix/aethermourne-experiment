## Appearance

A lean woman in her late forties with short-cropped dark hair streaked with white, sharp cheekbones, and grey eyes that have seen too many dead rise. Her armor is battered and practical, bearing scorch marks from undead encounters rather than ceremonial polish. She wears a necklace of [[Cosmology and Magic#Morrhaelite|Morrhaelite]] shards, each one taken from the skull of a Hollowdeep entity she personally destroyed. Her hands are scarred from cold-weather frostbite and blade work. She moves with the economy of someone who has learned to conserve energy in a frozen wasteland. She smells of woodsmoke, [[Cosmology and Magic#Morrhaelite|Morrhaelite]] dust, and the faint metallic scent of old blood. Her voice is rough from two decades of shouting orders into northern winds.

## Personality

First impression: grim, competent, exhausted. Ironholt is a pragmatist forged by twenty years of fighting an enemy that does not tire, does not retreat, and grows stronger with every casualty you suffer. She has little patience for posturing or ideology, and she measures every proposal against a simple question: will this help us hold the line in the north? Beneath the tactical focus is a woman who carries the weight of every garrison she has lost, every soldier who rose from their grave to fight her again, and the growing certainty that the Dominion is looking in the wrong direction while the real threat masses in the Hollowdeep.

## Backstory

Sera joined the [[Order of the Ashen Flame|Order]] at nineteen, assigned to the northern garrisons almost immediately. Her first winter at [[Pale Sentry]] killed half her unit through cold, starvation, and undead raids. She survived by being smarter, faster, and more willing to adapt unconventional tactics than her commanding officer. When he fell during a Black Frost incursion, she took command and held the garrison for three months without resupply.

She has commanded the northern frontier for eleven years, coordinating a network of garrisons, watch-posts, and ranger patrols that form the Dominion's only defense against [[The Pale Wastes|the Pale Wastes]]. She has worked closely with [[Thessa Ironveil|Commander Ironveil]] of the [[The Ashen Vigil|Ashen Vigil]], trading intelligence and coordinating patrols. The relationship is professional and respectful, two soldiers who understand that their orders and their reality do not always align.

Her advocacy for diplomacy with [[The Verdant Marches|the Marches]] is not idealism, it is cold arithmetic. If the Dominion commits the Ashguard to a western invasion, the northern garrisons will be stripped for reinforcements, the Hollowdeep will sense the weakness, and [[The Pale Wastes|the Pale Wastes]] will roll south like a tide. She has presented this analysis to [[Ashara Voss|Commander Voss]] seventeen times. Voss respects her judgment but prioritizes the Marcher threat.

## Relationships

- **[[Ashara Voss]]**, her Commander. Ironholt respects Voss's tactical brilliance but fundamentally disagrees with her strategic priorities. Their relationship is professional but strained.
- **[[Varen Ashfeld]]**, her rival. She considers Ashfeld a glory-seeking fool who has never held a frozen wall against a thousand shambling dead. Their War Council clashes are legendary and increasingly bitter.
- **[[Thessa Ironveil]]**, professional ally. Ironholt maintains regular correspondence with the [[The Ashen Vigil|Vigil's]] Commander, shares intelligence on Hollowdeep movements, and advocates for increased support to the Vigil within the Order's hierarchy.
- **[[Bjorn Ashken]]**, occasional contact. She has met the Holdwarden of [[Greyhold]] during coordinated border patrols and respects his pragmatism.
- **[[Aldren Voss]]**, sympathetic to his position. She understands why a garrison commander at [[Greyfield]] might question the Order's priorities, and she quietly advised him to be more careful when he declined the arrest warrants.

## What They Want

Ironholt wants the Order to recognize that the real threat is in the north, not the west, and to redirect resources to holding the line against the Hollowdeep before it is too late. Her deeper need is simpler and harder: she wants to stop losing people. Every soldier who dies on her watch and rises to fight her again is a failure she carries, and the weight is becoming unbearable. If the Order commits to a western war and the northern garrisons fall, she will have spent twenty years holding the line for nothing.

> [!warning]- GM Only
> **Campaign Role.** Voice of reason within the Order's leadership, potential ally for PCs navigating Dominion politics. She is the officer most likely to cooperate with non-Dominion factions if they can prove a common threat.
>
> **Key Secret.** Ironholt's correspondence with [[Thessa Ironveil]] includes intelligence she has not shared with [[Ashara Voss|Voss]] or [[Delric Mourne|the Cinders]], reports of organized undead movements in the Hollowdeep that suggest command-and-control far beyond normal necromantic activity. She suspects something is coordinating the dead and is gathering evidence before presenting it formally.
>
> **Arc Trajectory.** If the PCs bring evidence of [[The Twelve#Serith|Serith's]] influence or the Hollowdeep threat's true scale, Ironholt becomes a crucial ally within the Order's command structure. If the Ashguard marches west and the northern garrisons fall, she will lead a desperate rearguard action while blaming Voss and Ashfeld for strategic blindness.
>
> **Roleplay Notes.** Clipped, direct speech. No flowery language. Frequently references the north (e.g., "We don't have that luxury in the Pale Sentry"). Rubs her Morrhaelite necklace when thinking, a nervous habit. Sample line: *"You want to talk strategy? Fine. Draw me a map where the Ashguard marches west and the Hollowdeep stays quiet. I'll wait."*
