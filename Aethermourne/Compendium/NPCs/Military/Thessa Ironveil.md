## Appearance

A tall, gaunt human woman in her late forties with close-cropped grey hair and sharp blue eyes that hold contact a beat too long. Her face is lean and angular, hollowed by years of Lastlight's necromantic chill, and carries the flat, assessing gaze of someone who has spent too long looking at things no one should see. She wears the [[The Ashen Vigil|Vigil's]] black fur-and-bone armor with the precision of someone who treats her equipment as ritual. Twin [[Cosmology and Magic#Morrhaelite|Morrhaelite]] short swords hang at her hips, their black glass edges catching no light. She moves with controlled economy, every gesture deliberate, nothing wasted.

## Personality

Brilliant, haunted, and driven by a guilt she channels into competence. Thessa speaks rapidly and precisely, like someone who has rehearsed what she is going to say and resents the time it takes to say it. She is intensely private, uncomfortable with sentiment, and incapable of delegating danger. She will not send anyone into a situation she would not face herself, a principle that has kept her alive and earned her the [[The Ashen Vigil|Vigil's]] absolute loyalty. Beneath the control is a woman who keeps a private journal of every name she has lost and reads it before she sleeps.

## Backstory

Thessa grew up in [[Ember's Rest]], the Wastes' southern trade post, where she watched [[The Ashen Vigil|Vigil]] patrols return from the tundra diminished and silent. She enlisted at seventeen, driven not by idealism but by the cold arithmetic that the Vigil needed bodies and she had one to offer. She rose through the ranks on tactical ability and an unwillingness to die that bordered on stubbornness.

She took command after her predecessor, Commander Edric Hale, was killed during a routine patrol that turned into an ambush, the first sign that the [[The Pale Wastes#The Hollowdeep|Hollowdeep's]] undead were changing their behavior. The attack was coordinated. The dead set a trap. That should not be possible.

Since then, she has sent fifty-four scouts into the [[The Pale Wastes#The Hollowdeep|Hollowdeep]] to investigate the silence. None have returned. She has read every observation log in [[Lastlight]]'s Watcher Archive. She has watched the formations through [[Cosmology and Magic#Morrhaelite|Morrhaelite]] lenses herself: organized patrols, structures being built from bone and frozen earth, a skull whose energy signature has shifted from passive radiation to something rhythmic and purposeful. She is certain the Hollowdeep is preparing for war. She is equally certain that no one outside the Wastes will believe her until the army is already marching.

## Relationships

- **[[Bjorn Ashken]]**, mutual respect hardened by disagreement. She needs his fighters for the Hollowdeep expedition; he insists the Holds cannot be left undefended. Their arguments are fierce, honest, and necessary.
- **[[Merren Vask]]**, her best scout-captain, one of the fifty-four lost. Thessa carries Merren's name in her journal and the weight of having sent her in. The bone-message claiming Merren lives has shaken her more than she will admit.
- **[[Revenant Yael]]**, complicated respect. Thessa was raised in the Vigil tradition that all undead are enemies, but Yael's intelligence and willingness to fight have forced her to reconsider. She is not comfortable with the Remnants, but she is too pragmatic to refuse useful allies.
- **Scout-Captain Edda Voss** (lost), another of the fifty-four, now raised and serving as [[The Twelve#Serith|Serith's]] eyes on the crater rim. Thessa does not yet know this, but the figure in Vigil armor observed from the Ridge Posts is Edda using living communication protocols.

## What They Want

To understand what is happening in the [[The Pale Wastes#The Hollowdeep|Hollowdeep]] before it is too late, and to prove that the silence is a weapon being loaded, not peace. Deeper than that, she wants the fifty-four names in her journal to mean something, for their loss to have purchased knowledge that saves the living.

> [!warning]- GM Only
> **Campaign Role.** Military ally in Act IV. Thessa leads the expedition into the Hollowdeep, the campaign's largest dungeon. Her tactical brilliance makes her invaluable, but her willingness to sacrifice, including herself, makes her a danger. She is competent but shaken; [[Lastlight]]'s fall will be the worst defeat in the Vigil's history, and she will need the PCs to help her hold together.
>
> **Key Secret.** Thessa has seen the figure on the crater rim and recognized Edda Voss's Vigil field-sign for "hold position." She has told no one. She does not know whether the gesture means Edda is alive and waiting for rescue, or dead and baiting a trap. The uncertainty is destroying her.
>
> **Arc Trajectory.** Begins as a commander planning a desperate reconnaissance. After Lastlight falls, she becomes a soldier fighting a war she was not ready for. If the PCs support her and rally the Wastes, she transforms into the strategic mind that coordinates the defense. If unsupported, she leads a death-march into the Hollowdeep alone.
>
> **Roleplay Notes.** Speaks in clipped, precise sentences with no filler words. Makes eye contact that lasts uncomfortably long. Taps her sword hilts when thinking, a rhythm only she can hear. Never sits with her back to a door. Sample line: *"Fifty-four scouts. Fifty-four. I sent every one of them, and the crater swallowed them like they were nothing. I will not send more. I will go myself."*
