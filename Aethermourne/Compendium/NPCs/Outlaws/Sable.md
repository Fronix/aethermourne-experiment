## Description

Thirties, lean, dark-skinned, with close-cropped hair and a navigator's calluses on her hands. A thin scar runs along her jawline. She dresses like every other [[Driftmere]] chandler, practical clothes, salt-stained, unremarkable. Her eyes are the tell, too steady, too watchful, and in dim light they catch the light wrong, reflecting it back with a faint luminescence that she hides behind tinted lenses.

Sable was a [[Concord of Tides]] navigator before her "death at sea" five years ago. She was recruited by [[The Whisperer]] after surviving a Sleepwalker episode that took her into the water and brought her back changed. She does not hear whispers, she hears clarity. [[The Twelve#Serith|Serith]]'s voice, to her, sounds like truth spoken plainly. She believes the prison is an injustice and the Reach is built on lies. She is wrong, but she is competent and committed.

## Roleplaying Notes

- Calm, clipped speech with a navigator's precision
- Never uses her old name, reacts with hostility if confronted with it
- Wants [[Tidewall]] to burn for declaring her dead without searching for her

> [!warning]- GM Only
> Sable runs the [[Veil Unbound]]'s most active cell from the Pale Margin, a chandlery ship in [[Driftmere]]. She coordinates operations across the Reach, moving agents and materials through Driftmere's anonymous networks. She is [[The Whisperer]]'s most trusted lieutenant.
>
> Her cover in [[Driftmere]] is excellent. She can be identified through her luminescent eyes (a side effect of her submersion) or through her former [[Concord of Tides]] service records, which list her as dead.
