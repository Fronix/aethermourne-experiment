# The Whisperer

|||
|---|---|
| **Title** | Leader of the [[The Veil Unbound|Veil Unbound]] |
| **Location** | Unknown (moves between Reach cities) |
| **Faction** | [[The Veil Unbound]] |
| **Status** | Alive |

---

**Description.** Identity unknown, even to cult members. The Whisperer speaks with a voice that seems to come from everywhere and nowhere, a gift from [[The Twelve#Serith|Serith]]. They communicate through encoded messages, intermediaries, and occasional direct visits in which they appear as a figure wrapped entirely in dark cloth, face hidden. The Whisperer is fanatically devoted to Serith.

> [!warning]- GM Notes
> **Campaign Role.** The Veil Unbound's operational leader and a recurring antagonist throughout Act I and into later acts.
>
> **Identity.** Left for the GM to decide. Options include:
> - A Tidekeeper's relative (implicates the Concord's inner circle)
> - A former Lantern-Keeper (makes it personal for [[Lira Ashvane|Ashvane]])
> - A completely unknown figure (preserves mystery)
>
> Choose whichever option best fits your table's story.
>
> **Roleplay Notes.** Never raises their voice, the whisper is the point. Speaks in second person ("you will understand, in time"). Refers to Serith as "the truth beneath." Their presence is accompanied by a faint smell of deep brine.
