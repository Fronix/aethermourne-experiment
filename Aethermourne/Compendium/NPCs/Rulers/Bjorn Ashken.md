**Description.** A stocky, heavily scarred dwarf in his late hundreds (dwarves live long in Aethermourne) with a thick grey beard braided with bone beads and one blind eye (lost to an undead's claw decades ago). He wears fur-lined bone armor and carries a [[The Twelve#Morrhaeli|Morrhaelite]] axe. Bjorn is blunt, pragmatic, and exhausted. He has fought the dead his entire life and is preparing for what he fears will be the final battle.

> [!warning]- GM Notes
> **Campaign Role.** Primary authority figure in Act IV. He provides military support and logistical help. His pragmatic willingness to work with Remnants is crucial.
>
> **Roleplay Notes.** Speaks in short, declarative sentences. Doesn't waste words. Drinks bone-broth constantly (it's always cold in the Wastes). Shows affection through gruff practicality, if he gives you better gear, that's his version of a hug.
