# Brannoc Stillwater

**Marchwarden of Clan Stillwater** | [[Stillwater]] | [[Marchwardens Council]] | [[The Verdant Marches]]

---

## Appearance

Forties, heavyset, with a broad face and easy smile that makes people underestimate him. Dresses better than most Marchwardens (traded goods from [[The Hollowed Reach|the Reach]]). His hands are soft for a Marcher, he's a negotiator, not a warrior.

## Personality

Commercially minded, pragmatic, genial. Brannoc is the Marches' best trader and he knows it. He has connections with [[The Hollowed Reach|Reach]] merchants, [[The Ember Guild]], and the Mutagenists. He sees opportunity everywhere and ethics as flexible.

## What He Wants

- **Prosperity** for [[Stillwater]] and his clan.
- **A larger share** of the Marrow-Water trade.
- **Distance** from the unification debate, for as long as possible.

## Connections

- [[Marchwardens Council]], his political sphere
- [[The Ember Guild]], trade partners
- [[The Hollowed Reach]], merchant contacts
- [[Mirewatch]], Mutagenist trade

---

> [!warning]- GM Only
> Brannoc is selling Marrow-Water samples to Reach merchants without Greensinger approval, a serious cultural violation. He doesn't know (or care) what the buyers do with it.
>
> Some of his samples have ended up in [[Veil Unbound]] hands, used in rituals he'd be horrified to learn about. This creates a chain of consequence: Brannoc's greed fuels the Veil Unbound's experiments, which accelerate the very corruption threatening the Marches.
>
> **Plot hook:** Exposing this trade could shame Brannoc into compliance, or it could be used as leverage. PCs tracing Veil Unbound resources backward will eventually reach Stillwater.
