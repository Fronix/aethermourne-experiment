## Description

A man designed to be overlooked. Late fifties, thin, with silver hair combed back from a narrow face and deep-set eyes that give nothing away. He dresses conservatively in dark blues and moves quietly, easy to miss in a crowd, which is entirely deliberate. Cordell Raith is the [[Concord of Tides]]' intelligence chief, and he is genuinely good at his job. He processes information the way a mill processes grain: everything goes in, only useful output comes out. Calculating, patient, and outwardly competent, he is respected by his peers and feared by his subordinates. What he wants is simple: to survive what's coming. He has no illusions about the Reach's chances.

## Roleplaying Notes

- Speaks in measured, precise sentences
- Never volunteers information, only responds to direct questions
- Watches exits and entrances constantly, an old habit he doesn't bother hiding

> [!warning]- GM Only
> Raith is not a [[Veil Unbound]] member. He is a sympathizer. He has encountered [[The Twelve#Serith|Serith]]'s whispers through his intelligence work, interrogating Sleepwalkers, reading Whisper-Cell reports, and has privately concluded that Serith's release is inevitable. He has chosen to position himself for the world that comes after.
>
> He suppresses reports that might galvanize the [[Concord of Tides]] into action, ensures the [[Veil Unbound]] is never investigated too closely, and feeds information selectively, amplifying distractions and burying real threats.
>
> He is not evil like [[Delric Mourne]]. He is a coward making pragmatic survival calculations, and his cowardice is killing people.
