# Speaker Elowen Greaves

|||
|---|---|
| **Title** | Leader of the [[Unbound Congregation]] |
| **Location** | [[The Ashen Dominion#Ostivaar|Ostivaar]] (Lower Ribs district), [[The Ashen Dominion]] |
| **Faction** | [[Unbound Congregation|The Unbound Congregation]] |
| **Status** | Alive |

---

**Description.** A woman in her late thirties from the Hewn caste, with dark skin, warm eyes, and close-cropped hair. She wears simple homespun rather than the caste markers she's entitled to. Greaves is idealistic, eloquent, and tireless. She genuinely believes the Dominion can be reformed from within, that [[The Twelve#Solvaen|Solvaen]]'s true legacy is justice, not oppression. She is brave, principled, and possibly naive.

> [!warning]- GM Notes
> **Campaign Role.** Natural ally in Act II. She provides information, shelter, and political support. Her faith in reform is tested by the Cinders' depth of corruption.
>
> **Roleplay Notes.** Speaks with passion, leans forward, gestures broadly. Quotes Solvaen's early texts to counter the Church's orthodoxy. Listens earnestly. Uncomfortable with violence but not a coward.
