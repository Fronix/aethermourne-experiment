# Hierarch Valdren III

**Description.** An aging man in his late seventies, thin and sharp-featured, with grey-white hair and pale eyes that were once piercing but are now clouded with paranoia. He dresses in geometric white-and-grey robes and clutches an Ashite scepter. Valdren was once a reformer but has retreated into rigid conservatism and suspicious isolation. He trusts only [[The Cinders|the Cinders]], which is the worst possible choice.

> [!warning]- GM Notes
> **Campaign Role.** Tragic authority figure. His decisions (or inability to decide) shape the Dominion's fate in Act II. Exposing the Cinders' betrayal either galvanizes him or breaks him.
>
> **Roleplay Notes.** Speaks slowly, deliberately, pauses mid-sentence as if listening for eavesdroppers. Refers to himself in the plural ("we have decided"). Occasionally lucid and sharp, which makes the paranoia worse.
