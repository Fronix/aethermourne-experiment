# Kaelith Thornborn

|||
|---|---|
| **Title** | Aspirant Marchwarden / Unifier of the Marches |
| **Location** | [[The Verdant Marches#Thornhaven|Thornhaven]], [[The Verdant Marches]] |
| **Faction** | Thornborn Coalition ([[Marchwardens Council|Marchwardens' Council]]) |
| **Status** | Alive |

---

**Description.** A striking woman in her early thirties with dark brown skin, wild black hair threaded with small leaves (they grow there naturally since her awakening), and eyes that shift between green and gold. She bears a mark of green-gold on her right hand that pulses faintly. Kaelith is charismatic, fierce, and deeply uncertain beneath her confidence. She believes she's heard [[The Twelve#Thyrea|Thyrea]]'s voice, and that voice told her to unite the Marches. She's terrified of being wrong.

> [!warning]- GM Notes
> **Campaign Role.** Key ally in Act III. The PCs must determine whether her connection is genuine, help or hinder her unification, and journey with her into the deep Cradle.
>
> **Roleplay Notes.** Speaks quickly when excited, goes quiet when afraid. Touches the mark on her hand unconsciously. Alternates between inspiring conviction and private doubt, the PCs may see both sides.
