# Vael Sundren

**Mutagenist Chief Researcher** | [[Mirewatch]] | [[The Verdant Marches]]

---

## Appearance

Thirties, wiry, sharp features, restless eyes. Her left hand has webbed fingers, a controlled mutation for Marrow-Water handling. She wears stained leather work-clothes and smells of peat and chemical preservatives.

## Personality

Brilliant, pragmatic, anxious. Vael is a scientist in a world without the word. She observes, hypothesizes, tests, and draws conclusions with a rigor that would impress Dominion Calculists. She is terrified because her data is telling her something is changing in the Bogs and she cannot figure out what.

## What She Wants

- **Answers.** Why is Marrow-Water becoming more potent? Why the secondary emotional effect (subtle despair)? Why are the Bogs expanding?
- **Resources.** More researchers, better equipment, and someone who will listen to her findings without dismissing them as alarmism.

## Connections

- [[Mirewatch]], her laboratory and home
- [[Senna Deepmoss]], has quietly commissioned Vael to investigate the Bog expansion
- [[The Greensingers]], uneasy professional relationship
