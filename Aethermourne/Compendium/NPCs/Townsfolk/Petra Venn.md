# Petra Venn

**Unbound Congregation Organizer** | [[Ashveil]] | [[Unbound Congregation]]

---

## Appearance

Late twenties, slight build, dark hair worn loose (a deliberate violation of Keeper-caste grooming standards). Dresses as a student at the Athenaeum, though she was expelled two years ago. Her hands are ink-stained and she carries pamphlets in every pocket.

## Personality

Passionate, idealistic, reckless. Petra is the Congregation's most effective organizer in [[Ashveil]] because she genuinely doesn't care about personal risk. She distributes pamphlets, organizes secret meetings in Athenaeum cellars, and has built a network of sympathetic scholars and students.

She worships [[Elowen Greaves]] and has never met her. Greaves is in [[Ostivaar]].

## What She Wants

- **Caste abolition.** To prove that [[The Twelve#Solvaen|Solvaen's]] order was about justice, not hierarchy.
- **A document** in the Athenaeum's restricted collection that she believes contains pre-Church interpretations of Solvaen's teachings.

## Connections

- [[Unbound Congregation]], devoted organizer
- [[Elowen Greaves]], idol (never met)
- Athenaeum scholars and students, her network

---

> [!warning]- GM Only
> Petra is being watched by the Cinders, who plan to arrest her during the next "purge" and use her network to identify and eliminate Congregation sympathizers throughout [[Ashveil]]'s academic community.
>
> She is also, unknowingly, being protected by [[Ashara Voss]], who has instructed a trusted veteran in Ashveil to ensure Petra is warned before any arrest. Voss does not support the Congregation, but she opposes the Cinders on principle.
>
> **Plot hook:** PCs who discover the Cinder plan can warn Petra, earning the Congregation's trust. PCs who discover Voss's protection can use that leverage in dealings with the Marshal.
