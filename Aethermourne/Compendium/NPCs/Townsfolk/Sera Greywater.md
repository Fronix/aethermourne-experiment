## Description

Late thirties, sharp-featured, with dark hair cut at her jawline and watchful brown eyes. She dresses in the Concord's official blue-grey but adds personal touches: a good belt, polished boots, a silver ring that belonged to her mother. She carries herself like someone who expects to be underestimated and enjoys proving people wrong.

Ambitious, clever, and restless. Sera was appointed Toll-Warden as a minor patronage position, but she's turned [[Bridgemere]] into a far more profitable chokepoint than the Concord expected. She has a talent for leverage, knowing where the money flows and who can't afford to have it stop. She is not corrupt in the venal sense, she doesn't steal. She's corrupt in the structural sense, she uses her position to accumulate influence and information that she shouldn't have.

**What she wants:** Power. Specifically, a seat in [[Tidewall|Tidewall's]] political structure. [[Bridgemere]] is a stepping stone. The bridge crisis is an opportunity.

> [!warning]- GM Only
> Sera has been hiring private investigators in [[Tidewall]] to look into the Concord's bridge maintenance budget. She suspects the money is being diverted.
>
> She's right, but the trail leads not to simple corruption but to [[Cordell Raith]], who has been suppressing infrastructure reports as part of his broader strategy of enabling inaction.
>
> If Sera's investigation continues, she will eventually uncover evidence of Raith's treachery, which makes her both a potential ally and a potential target.
