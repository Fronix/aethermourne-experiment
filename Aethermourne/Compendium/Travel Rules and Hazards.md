# Travel Rules and Hazards

*Practical GM reference for overland travel in Aethermourne. All mechanics use the Nimble TTRPG system.*

---

## Movement Speed

| Travel Type | Distance/Day | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Road (on foot) | 24 miles | Maintained roads (Dominion, Emberveil) |
| Trail (on foot) | 18 miles | Marked paths, bone-cairn routes |
| Off-trail (on foot) | 12 miles | Unmarked terrain, forest, tundra |
| Difficult terrain | 8 miles | Swamp, deep snow, mountain passes, Dense Fen |
| Mounted (road) | 36 miles | Standard horses or equivalent |
| Mounted (off-trail) | 24 miles | Frost-elk in Wastes, riding beasts |
| Barge (waterway) | 30 miles | Greymere Sluice, river routes |
| Sailing (coastal) | 60 miles | Fair weather, known waters |
| Sailing (open ocean) | 80 miles | Fair weather, experienced crew |

**Forced March:** +8 miles/day. DC 13 Str save at end of day or gain 1 exhaustion level. Each consecutive forced march day increases DC by 2.

## Regional Travel Hazards

### The Ashen Dominion

| Hazard | Trigger | Effect (Nimble) |
|---|---|---|
| The Hum | Within 1 mile of Spine | Disadvantage on creative/improvisational checks. Will DC 12 to resist compulsive ordering behavior. |
| Order Sickness (exposure) | 8+ hours near raw Ashite | DC 13 Will save/day. Failure: -1 to all non-Calculism checks (cumulative, max -3). Resets after 3 days away from Ashite. |
| Cinder Patrols | Roads, settlements | Social encounter. Papers checked. DC 14 Will to resist interrogation. Foreigners flagged. |
| Calcified Forest | Off-trail eastern Dominion | Sound carries strangely. Perception DCs +2. Getting lost: DC 14 Will or lose 1d4 hours. |

### The Hollowed Reach

| Hazard | Trigger | Effect (Nimble) |
|---|---|---|
| Black Tide | Coastal areas (increasing) | Sea turns dark. Magic surges: spells have 1-in-6 chance of wild effect. Trench creatures surface. |
| Tidal Bridge Failure | Bridge crossings | DC 12 Dex to cross safely during flicker. Failure: fall into sea, 2d6 bludgeoning + current. |
| Fog ("The Breath") | Shattered Coast, all times | Visibility 60 ft. Navigation DC 13. Sounds distorted (+2 to enemy Stealth). |
| Sleepwalker Encounter | Coastal nights | DC 14 Will or hear whispers. Failure by 5+: compelled to walk toward sea for 1 round. |

### The Pale Wastes

| Hazard | Trigger | Effect (Nimble) |
|---|---|---|
| Bone-Pull | Within 500 ft of Marrow Field | Escalating Will saves: DC 11 (fatigue), DC 13 (depression, lose motivation), DC 15 (acceptance, stop moving). Morrhaelite equipment: advantage. |
| Bone-Storm | Random (40% increase) | 3 rounds: Death-Sight (no save, see spirits), Necrotic Wind (DC 13 Str, 2d6 necrotic), Whispers (DC 15 Will, Serith's voice). |
| Supernatural Cold | Constant | DC 12 Str save per 4 hours without cold-weather gear. Failure: 1d4 cold damage + 1 exhaustion. Near Hollowdeep: DC 14. |
| Undead Patrols | Between Holds, near crater | Silent, disciplined. Stealth DC 14 to avoid. If spotted, 1d4 rounds before reinforcements. |

### The Greymere Fenlands

| Hazard | Trigger | Effect (Nimble) |
|---|---|---|
| Ley Surge | Random (1-in-6 per hour in Nethys Pools) | All spells within 100 ft: roll 1d6. 1-2: spell fails. 3-4: spell succeeds with altered effect (GM choice). 5-6: spell succeeds at double potency. |
| Soft Ground | Off-trail travel | DC 12 Dex or sink to waist. DC 14 Str to pull free. Failure by 5+: sinking continues, 1 round to rescue. |
| Disorientation | Deep Fen | DC 14 Will per hour. Failure: lose 1d4 hours. Three consecutive failures: lost, require rescue or DC 16 to self-orient. |
| Bioluminescent Spores | Nethys Pools clearings | DC 13 Will or become fascinated (1d10 min, unable to willingly leave). Addictive: subsequent saves at -2. |

### The Verdant Marches

| Hazard | Trigger | Effect (Nimble) |
|---|---|---|
| Marrow-Water | Contact with divine-essence pools | DC 14 Str save. Failure: 1d6 necrotic + mutation (GM table). Heavy exposure: Rootbound transformation begins (3 saves over 3 days to resist). |
| Pollen Intoxication | Canopy level, Bloom Peaks | DC 12 Will per hour. Failure: drowsy (-2 Perception, disadvantage on initiative). 3+ failures: sleep 1d4 hours. |
| Predatory Flora | Off-trail travel | Vines reach for heat. DC 12 Dex or restrained (DC 14 Str to escape). Flowers: soporific burst, DC 13 Will or sleep 1 round. |
| Canopy Navigation | Mid-Canopy/Crown travel | DC 13 Dex for climbing/traversal. Failure: fall (2d6 per canopy level). Rope and climbing gear: advantage. |

### The Ironmarch

| Hazard | Trigger | Effect (Nimble) |
|---|---|---|
| War-Essence | Within 200 ft of fragments | DC 13 Will save per hour. Failure: rising aggression, -2 to social checks, advantage on attack damage but disadvantage on retreat/defensive actions. |
| Monolith Proximity | Within 200 ft of Shardfall monolith | Teeth-ache, flaring temper. DC 14 Will or provoke nearest creature (including allies) with hostile speech/action. |
| Iron Dust | Rust Coast, windy days | DC 12 Str save or 1d4 damage (inhaled iron particles). Extended exposure: voice rasps, -2 to Persuasion for 1d4 days. |
| Fragment Fields (sea) | Coastal sailing | DC 14 Dex (pilot) to navigate submerged iron fragments. Failure: hull breach, 1d6 hours to repair. |

### The Emberveil

| Hazard | Trigger | Effect (Nimble) |
|---|---|---|
| Geothermal Vent | Random (1-in-8 per hour in Caldera Ring) | DC 13 Dex to dodge. Failure: 2d6 fire damage. Steam cloud: 30 ft radius, heavily obscured for 30 seconds. |
| Volcanic Ash | Ashfall Slopes | -2 Perception (visual). DC 12 Str per 4 hours or 1 exhaustion (breathing difficulty). Cloth mask negates. |
| Unstable Ground | Deep Warrens, mine shafts | DC 13 Dex or floor collapses. Fall: 2d6 per 10 ft. Mine supports: DC 14 Perception to spot weakness. |
| Heat Exhaustion | Caldera Ring, summer | DC 12 Str per 4 hours without water. Failure: 1 exhaustion. Heavy armor: DC increases by 2. |

### The Thornwild

| Hazard | Trigger | Effect (Nimble) |
|---|---|---|
| The Cycle: Bloom | Growth phase (2-6 hours) | Terrain shifts as vegetation erupts. DC 13 Dex or 1d6 bludgeoning (root burst). Previously clear paths blocked. |
| The Cycle: Wilt | Death phase (2-6 hours) | Necrotic pollen fills air. DC 12 Str per hour or 1d4 necrotic. Undead in area gain +2 to all rolls. |
| Accelerating Cycle | Deep Thornwild | Phases compress to 1-2 hours. All Cycle DCs increase by 2. Rest impossible during Bloom/Wilt. |
| Thornwild Amber Contact | Handling raw amber | DC 14 Will. Failure: vivid hallucination of own death and rebirth. 1d4 rounds incapacitated. Success: brief flash of prophetic insight (GM's choice). |

### The Shattered Edge

| Hazard | Trigger | Effect (Nimble) |
|---|---|---|
| Reality Distortion | Threshold zone and deeper | Physics unreliable. Ranged attacks: 1-in-4 chance of trajectory shift (reroll direction randomly). |
| Gravity Shift | Shimmer Zone | DC 13 Dex per hour. Failure: gravity reverses locally for 1 round. Fall "upward" 10 ft, then return. 1d6 damage. |
| Maelstrom Surge | Random (increasing) | Zone expands rapidly. DC 15 Dex to flee expanding edge. Caught: DC 14 Will or gain 1 mutation (Changed transformation begins). |
| Time Inconsistency | All zones past Stable Ring | 1-in-6 chance per hour: lose or gain 1d4 hours. Party members may experience different durations. |

### The Unwritten Lands

| Hazard | Trigger | Effect (Nimble) |
|---|---|---|
| Black Sand (Veilshore) | Walking on sand after dark | DC 13 Will or hear whispers. Equipment left on sand: 1-in-4 chance missing by morning. |
| Fernwald | Entering without Oldborn guide | Forest shifts. DC 16 Will to maintain direction. Failure: path closes behind, rerouted to Fernwald's choosing. |
| Gyre-Wraith Proximity | Near Primordia or Shimmer | DC 14 Will or 1d4 psychic (body forgets shape momentarily). Aetheric magic: amplifies Wraith (feeds it). |
| Divine Residue Concentration | Outlander groups of 5+ | Primordial responses increase. Black sand analysis events. Anchor instability. Oldborn tension rises. |

## Rest Mechanics

| Rest Type | Duration | Recovery | Conditions |
|---|---|---|---|
| Short Rest | 1 hour | Recover abilities per Nimble rules | Must be out of immediate danger |
| Long Rest | 8 hours | Full HP, remove 1 exhaustion | Shelter required. See regional modifiers below. |
| Unsafe Rest | 8 hours | Half HP, no exhaustion removal | No shelter, active hazard zone. Roll encounter check. |

### Regional Rest Modifiers

| Region | Modifier |
|---|---|
| Ashen Dominion (roads) | Normal rest. Inns at regular intervals. |
| Ashen Dominion (Spine) | The Hum disrupts sleep. DC 11 Will or long rest counts as unsafe rest. |
| Hollowed Reach (coastal) | Sleepwalker risk during rest. DC 12 Will or wake exhausted (bad dreams). |
| Pale Wastes | Long rest requires shelter AND fire. Without fire: unsafe rest. Near Marrow Field: no rest possible (bone-pull). |
| Greymere Fenlands | Ley surges disrupt magical recovery. 1-in-6 chance during rest: all spell slots/abilities reset randomly (some restored, some not). |
| Verdant Marches | Pollen makes rest easy (too easy). DC 12 Will or oversleep by 1d4 hours. |
| Ironmarch (near fragments) | War-essence dreams. DC 13 Will or wake hostile (disadvantage on social checks for 1 hour). |
| Emberveil | Heat disrupts rest in Caldera Ring without cooling measures. DC 12 Str or long rest counts as unsafe. |
| Thornwild | Rest only possible during Bloom or Rise phase. Wilt/Cycle transitions wake sleepers. |
| Shattered Edge | Time inconsistency. Rest may take 6 hours or 12 hours (roll 1d6+5). |
| Unwritten Lands | Primordial dreams. Vivid, alien, not harmful but unsettling. First week: DC 12 Will or no rest benefit. Acclimates after. |
