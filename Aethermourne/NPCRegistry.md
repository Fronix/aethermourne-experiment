## NPC Relationship Map

> [!info] At a Glance, Key Relationships
> **Act I (Hollowed Reach)**
> - **[[Lira Ashvane\|Ashvane]]** trusts the PCs, tasks them directly
> - **[[Maren Selk\|Selk]]** must be convinced; responds to evidence and leverage
> - **[[The Whisperer]]** opposes the PCs from the shadows
> - **[[Celestine Vael\|Celestine]]** provides intelligence on the cosmic threat
>
> **Act II (Ashen Dominion)**
> - **[[Delric Mourne\|Mourne]]** is the hidden enemy; appears as an ally
> - **[[Hierarch Valdren III\|Valdren]]** is the key to the Dominion's response; fragile
> - **[[Ashara Voss\|Voss]]** can be turned if shown proof of Cinder corruption
> - **[[Elowen Greaves\|Greaves]]** is a natural ally; provides ground-level support
>
> **Act III (Verdant Marches)**
> - **[[Kaelith Thornborn\|Kaelith]]** needs the PCs' help to unify the Marches
> - **[[Elder Rosk\|Rosk]]** guides them toward the corruption's source
> - **[[Draven Ironbark\|Ironbark]]** must be won through deeds, not diplomacy
>
> **Act IV (Pale Wastes)**
> - **[[Bjorn Ashken\|Bjorn]]** provides infrastructure; pragmatic partnership
> - **[[Thessa Ironveil\|Ironveil]]** leads the military expedition; high stakes
> - **[[Revenant Yael\|Yael]]** is the moral question made flesh (or lack thereof)
>
> **Act V (The Second Silence)**
> - **[[The Twelve#Serith\|Serith]]** is the final confrontation, argument as much as battle

> [!warning] Naming Conventions
> - **Hierarch / Thane / Speaker**, Ashen Dominion titles
> - **Marchwarden / Elder / Warden**, Verdant Marches titles
> - **Tidekeeper / Keeper**, Hollowed Reach titles
> - **Holdwarden / Commander / Revenant**, Pale Wastes titles
> - **Shipwright / Warden (Toll) / Elder**, minor Hollowed Reach titles
> - All divine figures are referred to by name and epithet (e.g., "Serith, the Veiled One")
