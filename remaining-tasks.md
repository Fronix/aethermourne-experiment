**2. Add a new section: "Beyond Creation: The Outer Planes" (insert after the Current Cosmology section, before "Magic in Aethermourne")**

This section should be approximately 60-80 lines and cover:

- **Introduction** (2-3 paragraphs): The Three Planes were not ALL that existed. They were the portion of reality The Twelve claimed and shaped. Beyond those boundaries lie planes far older and more alien. Two have been identified through fragmentary scholarship: [[The Gyre]] (below/within) and [[The Fathom]] (beyond/outside).

- **The Cosmological Stack** (info callout with the layer diagram):
  - The Fathom (outside, surrounds everything)
    - The Boundary (celestial sphere, the stars)
      - The Shattered Empyrean (divine realm, collapsed)
        - Aethermourne (mortal world)
          - The Gyre (within/below, raw substrate)
  - The Loom of Fate threads through all layers

- **The Gyre** (brief summary, 1-2 paragraphs): The primordial substrate. Raw potential from which The Twelve drew material to build creation. The Aetheric Web filters Gyre-potential into usable magic. The Maelstrom is a breach. Home of vast forces called [[The Primordials]]. Link to `[[The Gyre]]` for full article.

- **The Fathom** (brief summary, 1-2 paragraphs): The alien beyond. What exists outside creation's boundary. The stars are structural anchors maintaining the separation. The boundary is weakening. Home of alien entities documented in `[[The Primordials]]`. Link to `[[The Fathom]]` for full article.

- **The Primordials** (brief summary, 1 paragraph): Forces and entities that predate The Twelve. Five identified in The Gyre, five in The Fathom. They are not gods, they are vast processes and alien intelligences. Link to `[[The Primordials]]` for the full catalog.

- **The Boundary** (brief summary, 1-2 paragraphs): The celestial sphere. Built by The Twelve using the principle of separation. Stars are anchor points. Currently degrading as divine maintenance fails. Link to `[[The Boundary Architecture]]` for structural details.

- **Connection to the Second Silence** (1 paragraph): The Second Silence is not just magical decline. It is the failure of every divine mechanism simultaneously: the Aetheric Web (magic), the death-cycle (Morrhael), the Loom (fate), AND the Boundary (cosmological containment). The outer planes add urgency: even solving Serith doesn't fix the Boundary.

- **GM callout** (collapsed): Note that most of this is unknown to mortals in-world. The Star-Readers have fragments. Lysara Shimmerborn suspects. The Lantern-Keeper Divers have sealed reports. But no faction has assembled the full picture. Players discovering this is campaign-defining.
