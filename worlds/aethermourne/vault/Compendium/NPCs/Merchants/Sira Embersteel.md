## Appearance

A compact, muscular woman in her mid-thirties with dark brown skin, close-cropped black hair, and burn scars laddering both forearms from wrist to elbow, the marks of twenty years at the forge. Her hands are large for her frame, calloused and steady, the hands of someone who shapes metal the way other people breathe. She wears a heavy leather apron over simple clothing, and her eyes are an unusual amber color, the only sign of [[The Twelve#Kaevroth|Kaevroth]] contamination she displays, though whether the color is contamination or simply hers, no one is certain. She smells of hot metal and coal smoke. She does not smile often, and when she does, it never reaches her eyes.

## Personality

Sira is quiet, focused, and haunted by a guilt she cannot resolve. She is the finest weaponsmith in Aethermourne, and she knows it, not with arrogance but with the matter-of-fact certainty of someone who has tested her work against every competitor and found no equal. The problem is what her work does. Blades forged from [[Cosmology and Magic#Kaevroth's Iron|Kaevroth's Iron]], especially the Monolith-proximate iron she works with, amplify aggression in their wielders. Every weapon she creates is a vector for contamination. She forges them anyway, because if she does not, someone less skilled will, and their blades will be worse, cruder, more dangerous to their wielders. It is a rationalization she returns to every night and finds less convincing each time.

## Backstory

Sira was born in [[Wrathspire]] to a family of smiths. Her grandmother worked the Singing Forges, as did her mother, and Sira was shaping iron before she could write her name. Her talent was evident early. By sixteen, she was producing blades that matched her mother's quality. By twenty, she surpassed her. By twenty-five, she was acknowledged as the finest smith in the Ironmarch. Now, at thirty-five, she is the finest smith on the continent, and the weight of that distinction is crushing her.

Three years ago, she forged a greatsword for a warlord named Torren Ashfall. The blade was her finest work, perfectly balanced, indestructible, beautiful. Within six months, Torren had massacred two settlements and killed his own family. When he was finally brought down, they found the blade fused to his hand, the iron had grown into his flesh. Sira keeps a sketch of the blade in her workshop. She looks at it when she needs to remember why she should be afraid of what she makes.

## Relationships

- [[Factor Gellen Ashworth]], [[The Ember Guild]]'s monitor at [[Wrathspire]]. He has tried to recruit her into the Guild three times. She refuses because joining would mean submitting her work to the Guild's regulatory framework, and she believes their containment research is too slow and too cautious.
- [[Mira the Red]], who has sent agents to secure Sira's services. Sira refused and now keeps a loaded crossbow near her forge.
- **Forge-Warden Hask**, Wrathspire's security chief. A friend who checks on her daily, partly out of concern and partly because Sira has been working longer hours near the Monolith than anyone should.
- [[Yrsa Bloodhand]], who commissioned a sword from Sira five years ago and then never collected it. Sira suspects the Iron Thane changed her mind about carrying a [[The Twelve#Kaevroth|Kaevroth]] blade. She is right.

## What They Want

Sira wants to forge a blade from [[Cosmology and Magic#Kaevroth's Iron|Kaevroth's Iron]] that does not corrupt its wielder. A weapon that carries all the metal's extraordinary properties, the unbreakable edge, the resonant power, without the war-essence contamination. She has been working on this for three years and has failed every time. Her deeper need is absolution: she wants proof that her craft can create something that helps rather than harms. Without that proof, she is not sure she can continue.

> [!warning]- GM Only
> **Campaign Role.** Key resource NPC in Acts II-III. She can forge weapons and tools from divine materials that the PCs need, and her expertise with [[The Twelve#Kaevroth|Kaevroth's]] iron is unmatched. She is also a potential ally in understanding the fragment migration.
>
> **Key Secret.** Sira has already come close to forging an uncorrupted blade, once. She used a sliver of [[Cosmology and Magic#Ashite|Ashite]], order-essence, mixed into the [[Cosmology and Magic#Kaevroth's Iron|Kaevroth's Iron]] during the forging process. The resulting blade was stable for two weeks before the war-essence overwhelmed the order-essence and the blade shattered. She needs more Ashite, or a different stabilizing agent, to try again. The [[The Twelve#Thyrea|Thyrea]] fragment beneath [[Duskfield]], if she knew about it, would be exactly what she needs.
>
> **Arc Trajectory.** If the PCs connect her with the [[Duskfield]] fragment or bring her sufficient [[Cosmology and Magic#Ashite|Ashite]], she can forge a weapon capable of disrupting [[The Twelve#Kaevroth|Kaevroth's]] fragment migration, a blade that dampens war-essence instead of amplifying it. This weapon would be critical in confronting [[Mira the Red]] or containing the Monolith. Without PC intervention, Sira will work herself to exhaustion near the Monolith and succumb to contamination within a year.
>
> **Roleplay Notes.** Speaks slowly, choosing words the way she chooses hammer strikes. Long silences mid-conversation are normal. Fidgets with a piece of raw iron she keeps in her pocket, turning it over in her fingers. Becomes animated only when discussing metallurgy.
