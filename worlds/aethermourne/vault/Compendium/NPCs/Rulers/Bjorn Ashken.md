# Holdwarden Bjorn Ashken

## Appearance

A stocky, broad-shouldered human man in his late fifties with a thick grey beard braided with bone beads. A long scar runs across the left side of his face, a memento from a bone-wraith encounter twenty years ago that killed his predecessor and half the Hold's council. One eye is clouded and blind from the same attack. He wears fur-lined bone armor out of habit rather than ceremony, and carries a [[Cosmology and Magic#Morrhaelite|Morrhaelite]] axe that has seen more use than any weapon in [[Greyhold]]. His hands are cracked from cold, his knuckles swollen. He smells of bone-broth and hearth smoke.

## Personality

Blunt, pragmatic, and exhausted in a way that has become permanent. Bjorn does not give speeches. He gives orders, explanations when pressed, and silences that communicate more than most leaders' rhetoric. He has no patience for anything that does not directly contribute to keeping his people alive, and he treats diplomacy as a necessary inconvenience rather than a skill. Beneath the grim exterior is a man who cares deeply but has learned that showing it costs energy he cannot spare. He shows affection through gruff practicality: if he gives you better gear, that is his version of warmth.

## Backstory

Bjorn was born in [[Greyhold]] and has never left [[The Pale Wastes]]. He grew up in the shadow of the [[The Pale Wastes#The Hollowdeep|Hollowdeep]], learned to fight before he learned to read, and joined the Hold militia at sixteen. He was competent, reliable, and unremarkable, the kind of soldier who survives because he never does anything reckless.

Twenty years ago, a bone-wraith incursion breached [[Greyhold]]'s outer wall and tore through the council chambers during a meeting. Holdwarden Saren Greymark and six of the eight council members died. Bjorn, a militia captain at the time, organized the counterattack, sealed the breach, and held command through three days of fighting before the [[The Ashen Vigil|Vigil]] reinforcements arrived. When the crisis ended, he was the one still standing. No one elected him. No one needed to.

He has led [[Greyhold]] for two decades since, through worsening bone-storms, the loss of fifty-four [[The Ashen Vigil|Vigil]] scouts in the [[The Pale Wastes#The Hollowdeep|Hollowdeep]], the silence of [[Frostmere]], and the slow withdrawal of [[The Ashen Dominion|Dominion]] support. He chairs the Holdwardens' Council at [[The Pyre]] during the Great Burning, though the role carries no formal authority. He advocates openly for cooperation with the [[Remnants Court|Remnants]], not from idealism, but because he cannot afford the luxury of prejudice when the dead outnumber the living.

## Relationships

- **[[Thessa Ironveil]]**, respects her competence but clashes over troop allocation. She wants fighters for her Hollowdeep expedition; he insists the Holds cannot spare them. Both are right, and both know it.
- **[[Revenant Yael]]**, an unlikely but pragmatic alliance. Bjorn was among the first Holdwardens to support the Remnant Accords' expansion. He judges Yael by what she does, not by what she is.
- **[[Nara Frostmere]]**, her silence haunts him. They exchanged routine supply messages for years. Her last communication mentioned the shapes under the ice were more active. He did not send help fast enough.
- **Holdwarden Saren Greymark** (deceased), his predecessor, killed in the bone-wraith incursion. Bjorn keeps Saren's bone-mark pendant in his quarters. He has never worn it.

## What They Want

To keep his people alive long enough for someone to solve the problem he cannot solve. Bjorn knows the [[The Pale Wastes#The Hollowdeep|Hollowdeep]] is preparing for war. He knows the Holds cannot win alone. What he needs, and will never ask for directly, is someone to share the weight.

> [!warning]- GM Only
> **Campaign Role.** Primary authority figure in Act IV. Bjorn provides military infrastructure, logistical support, and political credibility when the PCs arrive in the Wastes. His pragmatic willingness to work with [[Remnants Court|Remnants]] and outsiders is crucial to rallying the Holds for war. He is the military backbone of the Wastes' defense.
>
> **Key Secret.** Bjorn received [[Merren Vask]]'s bone-carved message, *"Merren Vask lives. The quiet has teeth,"* weeks ago and has told no one except [[Thessa Ironveil]]. He is terrified that acting on it will cost lives the Holds cannot spare, and equally terrified that ignoring it will cost more.
>
> **Arc Trajectory.** Bjorn begins as a man holding a line he believes will break. If the PCs earn his trust and rally the Wastes successfully, he transforms into a war leader willing to commit everything to one decisive action. If they fail, he retreats into fortress mentality and the Holds fall one by one.
>
> **Roleplay Notes.** Speaks in short, declarative sentences. Does not waste words. Drinks bone-broth constantly, always from the same dented tin cup. Shows emotion through action, never speech. When stressed, he touches the scar on his face without realizing it. Sample line: *"You want my fighters. Tell me who replaces them on the wall while they're dying in that crater."*
