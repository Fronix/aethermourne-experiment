## Appearance

A young woman who flickers like a candle in wind. One moment she is fully present, sharp-featured, dark-haired, wearing the remnants of traveling clothes that have begun to gray at the edges. The next moment she is translucent, her outline bleeding into the iron air, her features smoothing toward the featureless mask of the Hollow Ones. She snaps back, solid again, gasping, clutching her own arms as if holding herself together physically. Her hands are metallic at the fingertips, the consumption creeping inward. She speaks her name constantly, a whispered mantra between every sentence.

## Personality

Terrified, defiant, and heartbreakingly ordinary. The Remnant is not a hero, not a scholar, not a warrior. She is a person fighting to remain a person in a place designed to dissolve personhood. She is kind when she remembers to be, sharp when fear takes over, and confused when the Consuming steals a memory and she has to reconstruct who she is from what remains. Her most defining trait is stubbornness: she will not stop saying her name, she will not stop correcting people who call her "the Remnant," she will not stop insisting that she is real.

## Backstory

Her name is Sable. She says it constantly because the Consuming takes what you stop reinforcing. She was a traveler, though she can no longer remember where she was traveling to or from. She entered the Iron Descent by accident, following a passage beneath [[Ostivaar]] that she thought was a mine shaft. By the time she understood where she was, she had already passed through levels she could not return through. She reached Level 8 weeks ago, maybe months. Time dissolves here along with everything else.

[[Mother Amalgam]] took her in, feeding identity-fuel to the walls to slow Sable's consumption. But the Consuming is patient, and Sable is losing ground. She has already forgotten her family's faces, her hometown's name, the reason she was traveling. What remains is her name, her stubbornness, and a growing terror that she is becoming the iron she walks on.

## Relationships

- [[Mother Amalgam]]: Her protector and, potentially, her fate. Sable fears that "joining" the Amalgam's collective is what Mother Amalgam truly intends, trading individual consumption for communal absorption. She is not entirely wrong.
- [[The Hollow King]]: She can see him from the Refuge, frozen on his throne. He is her future if the Consuming wins. She cannot stop looking at him.
- [[Vessel Maren]]: The scholar documenting what is happening to Sable. Maren's clinical detachment is both comforting and horrifying, it means someone is recording her, but it also means someone is watching her disappear and taking notes.
- [[The Hunger]]: It whispers to her more than to anyone else, because she is the one fighting hardest. It tells her that letting go will feel like relief. She believes it. That is why she keeps saying her name.

## What They Want

Sable wants to escape. She wants to walk back into sunlight and feel warm and remember everything she has forgotten. Her deeper need is simpler: she wants to matter. She wants proof that her existence, however small, was real and counted for something. She would settle for someone remembering her name after she is gone.

> [!warning]- GM Only
> **Campaign Role.** The Remnant is the emotional anchor of Level 8. She is the most human presence on the level, and her slow dissolution gives the party a ticking clock and a personal stake in the Consuming's horror. She is not a plot device; she is a person, and the players should feel the weight of that.
>
> **Key Secret.** Sable's name, spoken aloud in the Heart of Iron on Level 9, would create a momentary crack in the Consuming's hold, not enough to free her, but enough to prove that individual identity can resist Kaevroth's hunger. The Echo would notice. The Mercy would weep. It would matter.
>
> **Arc Trajectory.** Without intervention, Sable is consumed within days, becoming another face in the Gallery of the Merged. If the party helps her (feeding identity-fuel, carrying her name to Level 9, or destroying the Hollow King's throne), she can be saved, but "saved" in the Consuming is relative. She will never fully recover what she lost.
>
> **Roleplay Notes.** Whisper "Sable" between sentences. Lose track of what she was saying mid-thought, then snap back with fierce clarity. "My name is Sable. I was, I was telling you something. My name is Sable. The walls are, no. My name is Sable. I'm still here."
