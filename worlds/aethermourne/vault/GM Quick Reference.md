# GM Quick Reference

---

## Nimble Core Mechanics

| Mechanic | Rule |
|---|---|
| **Actions per turn** | 3 heroic actions (attack, cast, move, skill check) |
| **Reactions** | Defend (reduce damage), Interpose (push ally, take hit), opportunity attack |
| **Attack rolls** | None. Roll damage directly. Natural 1 = miss. Max on die = crit (exploding) |
| **Advantage** | Stacks in Nimble (each source adds benefit) |
| **Saves** | Str, Dex, Will only. Will combines Int/Wis/Cha. No Constitution (Str covers it) |

### Movement (Spaces)

| Spaces | Feet Equivalent |
|---|---|
| 2 | 10 ft |
| 3 | 15 ft |
| 4 | 20 ft |
| 5 | 25 ft |
| 6 (default) | 30 ft |
| 8 | 40 ft |
| 10 | 50 ft |
| 12 | 60 ft |

### Armor Categories

| Category | Effect |
|---|---|
| **None** | Takes full damage (dice + modifier) |
| **M (Medium)** | Dice damage only, ability modifiers ignored |
| **H (Heavy)** | Half damage from all sources (crits and vulnerabilities bypass) |

## NPC Reaction Table (d6)

| Roll | Reaction | Behavior |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Hostile | Actively opposes. May attack, betray, or obstruct. |
| 2 | Unfriendly | Distrustful. Provides minimum cooperation. Demands payment or proof. |
| 3 | Cautious | Wary but willing to talk. Needs persuasion (DC 12) for help. |
| 4 | Neutral | Transactional. Will trade information or services fairly. |
| 5 | Friendly | Helpful within reason. Offers rumors, warnings, or minor aid freely. |
| 6 | Allied | Actively assists. Volunteers information, resources, or protection. |

**Modifiers:** +1 if PCs share faction, -1 if opposing faction, +1 for gifts/favors, -1 for threats/intimidation, -2 in the Pale Wastes (general distrust), +1 in the Verdant Marches (communal culture).

## Quick Encounter Pacing

| Phase | Rounds | GM Action |
|---|---|---|
| Opening | 1-2 | Establish the threat, first exchange of blows |
| Escalation | 3-4 | Bloodied triggers, environmental shifts, reinforcements |
| Resolution | 5-6 | Last Stands fire, retreat or victory, consequences |

**Target encounter length:** 4-6 rounds for standard, 2-3 rounds for minor, 6-8 rounds for boss.
