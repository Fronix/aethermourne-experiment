# Kael Torrn

## Appearance

Kael is a lean young man in his early twenties with the wiry build of someone who works a barge for a living. He has dark hair, usually damp and tousled from river spray, and sharp green eyes that are always watching, always assessing. His hands are rough and calloused from hauling rope and cargo, and he wears simple canvas work clothes, patched and stained with river water and ore dust. He carries a worn leather satchel across his chest, stuffed with cargo manifests, supply tallies, and hidden messages. He moves quickly, purposefully, with the nervous energy of someone who knows they are carrying dangerous information and cannot afford to be caught. He smells like cold river water, rope tar, and sweat.

## Personality

Kael is loyal, earnest, and inexperienced. He believes deeply in what [[Senna Kade]] and [[Orrin Pike]] are trying to build, because he has seen both systems up close and hates both. He has watched his uncle [[Dace Torrn]] navigate the river trade for years, always scraping by, always at the mercy of cartel prices and guild fees, and he is determined to help build something different. But Kael is young, and he has never been interrogated, never been threatened, never faced real violence. He is brave because he does not yet understand what he could lose. Beneath this, he is terrified of letting people down. Senna and Orrin trust him with the coalition's most critical communications, and if he fails, if he breaks, if he gets caught, the entire network collapses. He does not talk about this fear, but it follows him on every trip.

## Backstory

Kael was born in [[Millford]], the son of a failed grain merchant who drank himself to death when Kael was twelve. His uncle [[Dace Torrn]] took him in, raising him on the river barges and teaching him the trade. Kael spent his teenage years hauling cargo between [[Millford]], [[Ashcrag]], and [[Ironhollow]], learning the routes, the customers, the rhythms of the highland trade. He saw how the [[Highland Merchant Cartel]] squeezed river traders on transport fees while the [[Forge Council]] squeezed farmers on market prices, and how the workers at both ends of the supply chain had nothing.

Two years ago, Kael was hauling ore from [[Ironhollow]] when he overheard [[Caden Wroth]] threatening a miner who was behind on debt. The miner begged for one more week. Caden refused and had him dragged off the barge. Kael asked his uncle what would happen to the man. Dace shrugged and said, "Same thing that happens to everyone who can't pay. He disappears into the system." That night, Kael met [[Senna Kade]] in a tavern and offered to carry messages. She tested him with small tasks for six months, then brought him into the coalition. He has been running messages between [[Senna Kade]] and [[Orrin Pike]] ever since.

The formative event: Eighteen months ago, Kael was stopped by cartel enforcers on the road to [[Ashcrag]]. They searched his cargo and found nothing, because he had hidden the messages in a false bottom in a grain sack. The enforcer told him, "Next time, we won't be so polite." Kael realized in that moment that this was not a game, that if he was caught, he would not be warned again. He has carried that fear ever since, but he has never stopped running.

## Relationships

- [[Senna Kade]] — Leader and mentor. Kael trusts Senna completely and would die for the cause she represents. She gave him purpose when he felt like his life was going nowhere, and he sees her as the future of the highlands. Senna values Kael's loyalty but worries he is too young, too inexperienced, and will break if the cartel captures him. She has not told him about [[Rennic Vale]], the suspected informant, because she fears Kael would confront him and blow operational security.
- [[Orrin Pike]] — Coalition leader. Kael has met Orrin a dozen times during message exchanges at the [[Halfway Burn Site]]. He respects Orrin's passion but thinks he talks too much, says too much, trusts too easily. Kael wishes the farmers' side of the coalition was as disciplined as the miners', but he keeps this opinion to himself.
- [[Dace Torrn]] — Uncle and father figure. Dace raised Kael after his father died, and Kael owes him everything. Dace does not know about Kael's work with the coalition, he thinks Kael is just hauling cargo. If Dace discovered the truth, he would beg Kael to stop, because he has seen too many people crushed by the system to believe resistance is possible. Kael has not told him because he cannot bear to disappoint him.
- [[Lira Venn]] — Fellow runner. Kael respects Lira's cautiousness and her refusal to formally join the coalition. She has warned him multiple times that he is too trusting, too visible, and will get himself killed. He thinks she is too cynical, but he listens to her advice because she has survived longer than most.
- [[Dessa Kord]] — Coalition strategist. Kael reports to Dessa when he delivers messages to [[Ironhollow]], and she debriefs him on who he saw, what he heard, any signs of cartel surveillance. He finds her intimidating, she sees things he misses and asks questions that make him realize how much danger he is in, but he trusts her judgment completely.

## What They Want

Kael wants the coalition to succeed. He wants [[Senna Kade]] and [[Orrin Pike]] to win, the strike to break the cartel and the council, and the highlands to become a place where people like his uncle [[Dace Torrn]] can work without being bled dry. He wants to prove that he is not just a kid running errands, that he is critical infrastructure, that the coalition depends on him. He wants to be brave.

What he needs is to understand that bravery is not the absence of fear, it is acting despite fear. He is terrified of being caught, and he has convinced himself that admitting this fear means he is weak. He needs to recognize that fear is not a failure, it is information, and if he does not acknowledge it, it will get him killed. He needs to learn when to run, when to hide, and when to burn the messages and disappear.

> [!warning]- GM Only
> **Campaign Role.** Kael is the coalition's lifeline, the messenger who keeps [[Senna Kade]] and [[Orrin Pike]] coordinated. PCs investigating the coalition, helping it, or hunting it will encounter Kael as a vulnerable point. If the PCs help him (teaching him countersurveillance, providing safe routes, or protecting him from cartel patrols), the coalition's communication becomes more secure. If they betray him or capture him, he will break under interrogation and reveal everything he knows.
>
> **Key Secret.** Kael keeps a journal hidden under a floorboard in his uncle's barge, a record of every message he has carried, every meeting he has witnessed, and every name he has learned. He tells himself it is insurance, a way to protect the coalition if something happens to him. In reality, it is a catastrophic security breach. If the cartel finds it, the entire coalition is exposed.
>
> **Arc Trajectory.** If unchallenged, Kael will continue running messages until he is caught or the strike happens. If caught, he will be interrogated by [[Caden Wroth]] or [[Tallow Breck]], and he will break within hours, revealing [[Senna Kade]], [[Orrin Pike]], and the entire coalition structure. If the PCs help him destroy the journal and improve his operational security, he survives. If the strike succeeds, he becomes a hero of the new order. If it fails, he flees the highlands or dies trying.
>
> **Roleplay Notes.** Kael speaks quickly when nervous, his words tumbling over each other. He makes eye contact when trying to convince someone he is trustworthy, looks away when he is scared. He fidgets with the strap of his satchel when he is waiting for a reply. When he is trying to act older than he is, his voice deepens and he speaks more slowly, but it sounds forced. Sample line: "I have been running this route for two years. I know every bend in the river, every patrol schedule, every place to hide if someone is following. Senna trusts me. Orrin trusts me. You can trust me too. I will not let you down. I swear it."
